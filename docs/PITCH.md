# Pitch

Teams lose value when operational signals, analysis, and execution live in separate tools—and trust collapses when agents act without control. MMM turns a goal and evidence into a typed plan, safe read-only investigation, and a measurable proposal with human approval at the moment of risk.

The demo is deliberately honest: partner modes use deterministic fixtures. The production path swaps in a live adapter without changing the control plane, approval policy, observability, or evaluation layer.
