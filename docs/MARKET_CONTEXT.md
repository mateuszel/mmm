# Market context

**Status: Source-backed benchmark plus provisional differentiation.** Accessed 2026-07-11.

## InPost Von Halsky — market benchmark

The official [Von Halsky page](https://inpost.pl/VonHalsky) presents an AI shopping assistant inside the InPost app. Publicly described capabilities include typing or speaking a need, searching offers available in the app, comparing prices and parameters, adding a selected item to the cart, choosing courier or Paczkomat delivery, and paying with a saved method. It demonstrates that conversational shopping, checkout and delivery are moving into mainstream consumer applications.

### Overlap

- Natural-language product intent.
- Offer discovery and comparison.
- A path from shopping to checkout and delivery.
- Consumer-grade experience rather than a developer console.

### Proposed Team MMM differentiation

- Private-seller evidence gathering and concise negotiation.
- Explicit shopping, communication and payment mandates.
- Detection of attempts to move payment outside a protected platform.
- Decision receipts explaining why the agent bought, asked or stopped.
- Measurable strike precision and false-buy behavior from the official Solidgate case.

### Correct future language

- `Market benchmark`
- `Comparable product direction`
- `Possible future logistics integration`

Do not say `partnership with InPost`, `integration with Von Halsky`, or `powered by InPost`. No InPost API or relationship is confirmed.
