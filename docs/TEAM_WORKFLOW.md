# Team workflow

Confirm ownership at the event; do not permanently assign people in advance.

Three workstreams: (1) product framing, agent behavior, business impact, and pitch; (2) frontend, interaction flow, dashboard, and demo polish; (3) runtime, tools, adapters, fixtures, and validation.

Use short-lived `feat/agent`, `feat/ui`, and `feat/demo` branches. Keep `main` deployable, integrate frequently, and avoid force pushes and long-lived branches.
