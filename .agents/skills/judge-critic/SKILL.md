# Judge critic

Read `docs/CURRENT_CHALLENGE.md`, `docs/PARTNER_RESEARCH.md`, `docs/PITCH.md`, and `docs/IDEA_BANK.md`. Score partner fit, user value, technical credibility, meaningful OpenAI use, business potential, and seven-hour feasibility. Distinguish official criteria from inferred lenses. List the five strongest judge objections with evidence, severity, and the smallest mitigation. Challenge unsupported claims, mock/live ambiguity, vague impact math, and features that cannot be demonstrated.
