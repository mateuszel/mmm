# Judge critic

Read `docs/OFFICIAL_CASE.md`, `docs/CURRENT_CONCEPT.md`, `docs/PRODUCT_BRIEF.md`, `docs/OPEN_DECISIONS.md`, and `docs/PRESENTATION_CONTEXT.md`. Score partner fit, user value, technical credibility, meaningful OpenAI use, business potential, demo clarity, and remaining-time feasibility. Distinguish confirmed requirements from provisional ideas. List the five strongest judge objections with evidence, severity, and the smallest mitigation. Challenge unsupported claims, mock/live ambiguity, unsafe negotiation or spending behavior, vague impact, and features that cannot be demonstrated in three minutes.
