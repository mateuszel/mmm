# Partner context

Read `docs/OFFICIAL_CASE.md`, `docs/HACKATHON_CONTEXT.md`, `docs/RESEARCH.md`, and `docs/OPEN_DECISIONS.md`. Verify current public facts against official sources and link each material fact. Label case-brief statements `Confirmed`, team ideas `Provisional`, and unsupported product availability such as ChatGPT Sites `Requires validation`. Reject unsupported integration, compliance, or judging claims. Keep mocked communication, commerce, and payment behavior described as mocked; never add credentials or secret-bearing examples.
